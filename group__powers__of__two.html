template<typename T >
T mpblocks::cuda::nextPow2 ( T  x)
inline

returns the smallest power of two that is not less than x

If x is a power of two, then it returns x. If it is not a power of two, it returns the next higher one.

For example: 

    x    prev     next
 ------ ------   ------
    0 :     0        0
    1 :     1        1
    2 :     2        2
    3 :     2        4
    4 :     4        4
    5 :     4        8
    6 :     4        8
    7 :     4        8
    8 :     8        8
